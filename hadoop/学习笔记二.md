##  hadoop安装

~~~
1.准备安装文件
~~~

hadoop-2.7.2.tar.gz

~~~
2.解压hadoop文件
~~~

tar -zxvf hadoop-2.7.2.tar.gz -C /opt/module/

~~~
3.配置hadoop环境变量
~~~

vim /etc/profile

##HADOOP_HOME
export HADOOP_HOME=/opt/module/hadoop-2.7.2
export PATH=$PATH:$HADOOP_HOME/bin
export PATH=$PATH:$HADOOP_HOME/sbin

~~~
4.刷新配置文件
~~~

source /etc/profile
